Sports Club

The Sports Club at Keystone School of Engineering encourages students to stay active and healthy through various sports and fitness activities. The club promotes teamwork, leadership, and discipline among students.

Activities and Events:

  • Inter-College Tournaments: Participation in various sports competitions across colleges, including cricket, football, basketball, and badminton.
  • Annual Sports Meet: A grand event where students showcase their athletic abilities in various track and field events.
  • Fitness Workshops: Special sessions on fitness, yoga, and wellness to promote a healthy lifestyle among students.
  • Friendly Matches: Organizing matches within the campus to boost camaraderie and sportsmanship.
  • Outdoor and Indoor Games: Facilities for various sports such as table tennis, volleyball, chess, and carrom are available for students.

The Sports Club provides an excellent platform for students to develop their physical and mental well-being while balancing their academic life.

Join Us!

If you're passionate about sports and fitness, the Sports Club is the perfect place to engage, compete, and stay fit!
