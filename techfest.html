Tech Fest

The Tech Fest at Keystone School of Engineering is an annual event that provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and innovations. It is a blend of competitions, workshops, and exhibitions that inspire creativity and problem-solving.

Event Highlights:

  • Project Exhibition: Innovative student projects across various engineering disciplines.
  • Coding Hackathons: Competitive coding events to challenge and enhance programming skills.
  • Robotics Competitions: Exciting challenges in robotics and automation.
  • Tech Talks: Expert sessions on emerging technologies such as AI, IoT, Blockchain, and Cloud Computing.
  • Startup Showcase: A platform for budding entrepreneurs to pitch their ideas and gain mentorship.

The Tech Fest fosters a spirit of innovation and technological advancement, encouraging students to think out of the box and stay updated with industry trends.

Why Participate?

Participating in the Tech Fest offers students exposure to the latest technological trends, networking opportunities with industry professionals, and a chance to win exciting prizes.

Join Us!

Gear up for an exciting journey of innovation and technology. We invite all students to participate and showcase their talents!
