Admissions

Courses Offered

Keystone School of Engineering offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the following disciplines:

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Mechanical Engineering
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Computer Science
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering

Fee Structure

The fee structure for undergraduate and postgraduate programs is as follows:

  • Undergraduate Programs: ₹1,00,000 per year
  • Postgraduate Programs: ₹1,20,000 per year
  • Hostel Fee: ₹50,000 per year
