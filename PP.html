Overview

Welcome to the campus placement portal. Here, we guide students through the recruitment process, ensuring they are prepared to step confidently into their professional journeys.

Placement Process

  1. Registration: Students must register on the placement portal with updated resumes.
  2. Aptitude Test: Companies conduct online or offline tests to assess analytical and problem-solving skills.
  3. Group Discussion: Shortlisted candidates participate in group discussions to evaluate communication and teamwork skills.
  4. Technical Interview: Candidates are assessed on their technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities.
  5. HR Interview: A final round to understand candidates' personality, goals, and suitability for the company.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 60% aggregate in academics (10th, 12th, and graduation).
  • No active backlogs at the time of application.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Specific criteria as per company requirements.
