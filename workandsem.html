Workshops & Seminars

At Keystone School of Engineering, workshops and seminars play a crucial role in bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical exposure. These events provide students with opportunities to learn from industry experts and gain insights into emerging technologies.

Key Workshops and Seminar Topics:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Hands-on experience with AI tools and frameworks.
  • Cyber Security: Understanding ethical hacking and security measures in the digital era.
  • Internet of Things (IoT): Building smart applications using IoT devices.
  • Entrepreneurship & Startups: Guidance on turning ideas into successful businesses.
  • Career Development: Soft skills, resume building, and interview preparation tips.

Our workshops and seminars are designed to provide students with a competitive edge in their professional careers and enhance their learning experience beyond the classroom.

Why Attend?

Attending these sessions helps students stay updated with the latest industry trends, interact with professionals, and gain practical exposure to current technologies.
