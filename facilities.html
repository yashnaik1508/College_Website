Library

The Keystone School of Engineering's library is a key resource for both students and faculty. It is well-stocked with a diverse collection of books, journals, research papers, and e-resources across various disciplines. The library provides students with access to essential learning materials, aiding in their academic growth. It offers a quiet and conducive environment for focused reading and study, along with computer terminals that allow students to access online resources and research databases. The library also hosts regular book fairs and events, enriching the overall educational experience.

Computer Labs

Our computer labs are designed to meet the technological needs of students across all engineering disciplines. Equipped with the latest software and high-performance hardware, the labs provide students with a hands-on learning experience in programming, data analysis, and engineering design. Whether it's running simulations, developing applications, or designing digital solutions, our computer labs support a wide range of academic and research activities. The labs are spacious and well-maintained, providing an ideal environment for both individual and group work, encouraging collaboration and creativity.

Seminar Hall

The Seminar Hall at Keystone School of Engineering is a modern, multipurpose space designed to facilitate academic and professional events. With state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment, the hall is used for guest lectures, seminars, workshops, conferences, and student presentations. It is equipped with comfortable seating and provides an excellent environment for interaction between experts from various fields and students. The Seminar Hall plays a vital role in bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical industry experience, giving students exposure to industry professionals and their insights.

Hostels

Keystone School of Engineering offers comfortable and secure hostel facilities to both male and female students. The hostels are equipped with essential amenities such as Wi-Fi, 24/7 electricity, water supply, and recreational areas. Rooms are spacious and furnished with necessary fixtures to ensure a comfortable living experience. The hostels provide a vibrant community environment, where students can engage in activities, build lasting friendships, and relax after a day of academic work. Safety and security are paramount, with 24-hour surveillance, ensuring students feel safe and at ease during their stay.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria at Keystone is the heart of campus life, offering a wide variety of nutritious and tasty food options. With a focus on healthy eating, the cafeteria serves balanced meals, snacks, and beverages to cater to diverse tastes and dietary requirements. It is an ideal space for students to socialize and unwind during breaks, providing a warm and inviting atmosphere. The cafeteria also promotes sustainability, offering eco-friendly practices such as waste segregation and the use of locally sourced ingredients. It plays a central role in fostering a sense of community on campus.

Transport Services

Keystone School of Engineering provides efficient and reliable transport services to ensure the safety and convenience of students and staff commuting to and from campus. The college operates a fleet of buses covering various routes throughout the city and surrounding areas. The transport system is designed to accommodate students' schedules, ensuring timely arrival for classes and activities. With well-maintained buses and dedicated staff, the transport service offers a hassle-free commuting experience, making it easier for students to focus on their academics without worrying about transportation logistics.
