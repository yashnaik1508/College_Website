Annual Day

Annual Day at Keystone School of Engineering is a much-awaited event that celebrates the achievements and talents of our students. The event is filled with cultural performances, awards, and a sense of togetherness.

Highlights of the Event:

  • Inauguration Ceremony: The event starts with an inspiring speech by our Principal, Dr. Sandeep Kadam, and Director, Prof. Y.R. Soman.
  • Cultural Performances: Students showcase their talents in dance, music, drama, and more.
  • Award Distribution: Felicitation of students excelling in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.
  • Fun Activities: Exciting games, food stalls, and interactive sessions for students and faculty.
  • Vote of Thanks: The event concludes with gratitude towards participants, faculty, and sponsors.

Annual Day is not just a celebration but a reflection of the hard work and achievements of our students, making it an unforgettable experience for everyone.

Join Us!

Be part of the celebrations and create memories that last a lifetime. We look forward to seeing you at our next Annual Day!
