About Us

College Overview

Keystone School of Engineering, established in 2005, has emerged as one of the leading institutions in the region for quality technical education. Located in the heart of Pune, the campus is equipped with modern facilities and offers both undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. The college strives to provide a world-class education and has built a strong reputation for academic excellence. With dedicated faculty, well-equipped laboratories, and a focus on research and development, Keystone aims to nurture skilled professionals ready to drive innovation and contribute meaningfully to society.

Vision & Mission

Vision:Keystone School of Engineering envisions becoming a leading institution in the realm of engineering education. The goal is to offer transformative learning experiences that inspire students to address global challenges. The college aims to cultivate the next generation of engineers who will lead the way in driving societal progress through innovative solutions and technical expertise. By focusing on forward-thinking educational practices, Keystone aims to empower students to meet the evolving demands of the global workforce.

Mission: Keystone School of Engineering’s mission is to provide an education that balances theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on learning. The institution is dedicated to fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities in its students. Keystone emphasizes the development of skills necessary to succeed in a fast-paced, technology-driven world. The college also strives to maintain an inclusive and supportive learning environment that encourages innovation, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning, preparing students for successful careers in engineering and related fields.

Leadership

Principal's Message

Dr. Sandeep Kadam, the Principal of Keystone School of Engineering, expresses his belief in the transformative power of education. He states, “At Keystone, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers, innovators, and thinkers. Our educational programs are designed to equip students with both academic knowledge and practical skills. We encourage our students to think critically, explore new ideas, and embrace challenges as opportunities for growth. Our mission is to help each student achieve their full potential and prepare them for success in their careers and in life.”

Management Team

The management team at Keystone School of Engineering plays a crucial role in steering the institution towards excellence. Under the guidance of Prof. Y.R. Soman, Director, the management team is dedicated to maintaining high academic standards, implementing innovative programs, and ensuring the overall development of students. The team works collaboratively to enhance the learning experience, foster a culture of research and innovation, and ensure that the college remains at the forefront of engineering education. Their combined efforts create an environment where students are motivated to excel academically and professionally.

Achievements and Milestones

  • Accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA)
  • Ranked among the top engineering colleges in the region
  • Successfully placed over 80% of our graduates in reputed companies
  • Organized several international conferences and workshops
  • An 'A' grade from NAAC reflects the institution's academic excellence.
